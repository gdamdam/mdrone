What it is

A serious instrument built around one sustained tone. Layered voices, slow weather, microtonal tuning, a long reverb tail, and two slow modulators — so a held note stays interesting for hours. No clock, no beats, no quantize.

works on iPhone, iPad, Android, desktop Free & Open Source No Install · No Account
