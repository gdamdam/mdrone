Hold a note. Shape the air.

Drone music for the browser.

mdrone is a real instrument for long tones, harmonic beds, drift, resonance, and sympathetic vibration. It was made for the strange calm that arrives when a sound is allowed to stay alive.

Pick a tonic. Build a timbre stack. Push it through weather, effects, tuning, and time. Save the scene. Share the whole thing as a link. No account wall. No install.

Why It Feels Different

Built for sustain, not for content.

It is a browser instrument designed around one thing: sustained sound with enough depth to stay interesting for a long time. That means fewer distractions, more listening, and controls that make sense when a note is allowed to breathe.

Play

Start from a preset or from a bare tonic and shape the room slowly while the drone is already alive.

  • 7 voice engines: tanpura, reed, metal, air, piano, FM, amp
  • Macros for drift, air, time, sub, bloom, and glide
  • Large weather surface for brightness and motion

Tune

The instrument is comfortable in just intonation, meantone, harmonics, maqam, slendro, and plain equal tuning.

  • 6 tuning systems and 6 interval relations
  • Per-interval fine detune, live
  • Partner drone and ritual journey modes

Keep

Save a scene locally, record the master, or pass the exact state to another listener with a link.

  • Named local sessions and autosave
  • Shareable URLs with scene state intact
  • Optional motion recording inside the link
Sound World

From sacred drones to damaged clouds.

The preset library moves across devotional string drones, harmonium and pipe-organ stillness, spectral long tones, ambient beds, and blown-out amplifier mass. It is not genre cosplay. It is a set of playable atmospheres shaped by real listening habits.

Voices and effects

Layer physically distinct voices, then push them through a serial chain that can stay delicate, cavernous, frozen, granular, or filthy depending on how far you lean.

Voices: tanpura, reed, metal, air, piano, FM, amp
Effects: tape, wow, sub, comb, ringmod, formant, delay, plate, hall, shimmer, freeze, cistern, granular, graincloud

Presets and motion

43 presets across sacred, minimal, organ, ambient, and noise territories. Then mutate, evolve, or send the scene through a ritual journey when you want the drone to keep moving on its own.

Presets: 43 authored scenes across 5 groups
Motion: evolve, mutate, journey, breathing LFO, recorded gestures
Views: drone, meditate, mixer
