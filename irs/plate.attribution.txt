Greg Hopkins EMT 140 Plate Reverb Impulse Response

Source:  https://oramics.github.io/sampled/IR/EMT140-Plate/
File:    emt_140_medium_1.wav (medium tone, shortest decay)
License: Creative Commons Attribution

Hosted in the oramics/sampled repository, originally from Hopkins
Media Services. The "medium" flavour is the most neutral of the
three tonalities (bright / medium / dark); _1 is the shortest
decay (~1 s). Picked for use as an alternative tail to mdrone's
algorithmic Dattorro fx-plate when authentic plate character is
preferred over the parametric model.
